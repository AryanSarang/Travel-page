Frequently asked questions

Do you provide assistance with visa applications and requirements?
Yes, we can provide guidance and support for visa applications if needed.
How do you ensure the safety of travelers during the pandemic?
We implement enhanced sanitation practices, social distancing measures, and adhere to local regulations to prioritize the safety of travelers.
Is travel insurance included in the packages?
While basic travel insurance might be included in some packages, we highly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to cover unforeseen circumstances and ensure your peace of mind.
What's the best time to visit the destination I'm interested in?
The best time to visit varies by destination. Our travel experts can provide you with information on optimal travel times.
What's the baggage allowance for flights included in the package?
Baggage allowances depend on the airline and the package. We'll provide you with this information upon booking.
Do I need to provide a negative COVID-19 test result before traveling?
Document requirements vary by destination. We'll provide you with the latest travel advisories and requirements.
